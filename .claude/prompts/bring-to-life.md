# Bring to Life

Enhance a feature to make it more engaging and help bring ancestors to life.

---

## Context

Feature: $ARGUMENTS

## Instructions

Review and enhance the feature to align with the "Bringing History to Life" differentiator.

### 1. Narrative Opportunities

Can we generate human-readable narratives?

```
Instead of:
  Birth: 15 Mar 1842, County Cork, Ireland

Consider:
  "John was born on March 15, 1842, in County Cork, Ireland,
   during the early years of the Great Famine."
```

### 2. Historical Context

Can we add relevant historical context?

- Major events during their lifetime
- What was happening in their location
- Typical life circumstances for their demographic

Data sources to consider:
- Wikipedia historical events API
- Historical occupation descriptions
- Immigration/emigration patterns

### 3. Visual Storytelling

Can we enhance visual presentation?

- Timeline visualizations
- Maps showing migration
- Photo galleries with context
- Family group portraits

### 4. Personal Touches

Can we make data feel more personal?

- "Today in history" for ancestors
- Anniversary reminders
- "On this day X years ago..."
- Age calculations ("would be 182 years old")

### 5. Discovery & Exploration

Can we encourage exploration?

- "You might also be interested in..."
- Related ancestors
- Shared experiences across family
- Patterns and trends

### 6. Sharing & Celebration

Can users share discoveries?

- Shareable cards/images
- Social media friendly formats
- Printable summaries
- Family newsletter content

## Questions to Ask

- What would make a user say "wow" when viewing this?
- How can we make this ancestor feel like a real person?
- What story is the data trying to tell?
- How would a family reunion presenter use this?

## Output

Provide:
1. Specific enhancements for the feature
2. Implementation suggestions
3. Content/copy examples
4. Any external data sources needed
